General Information

This project has the function of administration and registration of data of the area of ​​a warehouse specifically of the registration of products, registration of suppliers and employees, the program shall facilitate the handling of any data received from a product from a batch supplied by the supplier . It seeks to facilitate the access and management of the warehouse inventory. It is intended to cover the needs of the inventory of the minisuper with the tool that is focused on the registration and sample of data, seeking to have an organized inventory that allows the easy management of the company

Why is it important that all inventory is kept organized?

The objective of the inventory is to achieve that balance, as well as having reliability in the times of receipt of merchandise from the supplier as in delivery to customers.

The system proposes to have a function of administration and data registration of the storage area of ​​our suppliers, but specifically the registration of each product, a record of suppliers and each employee who uses our program on which will facilitate this work of handling all products.

System functionality

The program is capable of managing information from products and employees for ease of management and general knowledge of existing inventory.

It will be able to facilitate the entry and consultation of records within the functions of the company to emphasize the fast and safe work of the inventory organization: It seeks to facilitate access and handling of all data received such as: Information about the products: Name, description, date of preparation, expiration date, barcode, weight, quality and its category.

information about employees: DUI, first name, last name, mail, age, gender, address and telephone.Information about suppliers: name supplier and the company for which you work. Information about the lots purchased: date of purchase and the total of the products supplied by the lot.

List of actions available in programme I. The program has the ability to manage stored products II. The program will be able to manage the payroll of employees and the data of these III. The program has the ability to only allow you to do certain functions depending on the assigned user level IV. The program will only let employees who have a registered user V enter. The program will have a main page that will guide the user to the different sections available.
