General information

This project has the function of managing and registering data from the area of ​​a warehouse specifically the registration of products, registration of suppliers and employees, the program will facilitate the management of all data received from a product from a batch supplied by the supplier. It seeks to facilitate access and management of the warehouse inventory. It is intended to cover the needs of the inventory of the minisuper with the tool that focuses on the registration and data display, seeking to have an organized inventory that allows the easy management of the company

The objective of the inventory is to achieve that balance, as well as to have reliability in the times of receipt of merchandise from the supplier as well as in the delivery to customers.

The proposal to have a function of administration and data registration of the storage area of ​​our suppliers, but specifically the registration of each product, a record of the suppliers and each employee who uses our program which will facilitate this work of handling of all products.

System functionality

The program is capable of managing information coming from products and employees for the ease of management and general knowledge of the existing inventory.

It will be able to facilitate the entry and consultation of records within the functions of the company to emphasize the fast and safe work of inventory organization: It seeks to facilitate access and management of all data received such as: Information on the products: Name, description, production date, expiration date, barcode, weight, quality and its category.

Information about employees: DUI, first name, last name, mail, age, gender, address and telephone number. Information about suppliers: supplier name and company for which they work. Information on the lots purchased: date of acquisition and the total of the products supplied by the lot.

List of actions available in program I. The program has the ability to manage stored products II. The program will be able to manage the payroll of employees and their data. III. The program has the ability to only allow certain functions to be performed according to the assigned user level IV. The program will only allow employees who have a V registered user to enter. The program will have a main page that will guide the user to the different sections available.



Who benefits from the program?

The purpose of the system is to facilitate the organization of a supermarket warehouse, it seeks to manage an organized and controlled inventory of incoming products and outgoing products, as well as control of the lots supplied to the store. It will be able to facilitate the entry and consultation of records within the functions of the company to emphasize the fast and safe work of the inventory organization:

It seeks to facilitate access and management of all data received such as: Information about the products: Name, description, production date, expiration date, barcode, weight, quality and its category. Information about employees: DUI, first name, last name, mail, age, gender, address and telephone number. Information about suppliers: supplier name and company for which they work. Information on the lots purchased: date of acquisition and the total of the products supplied by the lot.

Therefore, the company benefits from the program, guaranteeing an order and organization of the company's inventory as well as billing control of suppliers, batches and products. The easy use and application of the system is guaranteed for the administration of all data received within this work area, taking data control with the best security.
